Privacy Policy

Handling of Personal Information

NanoFrontier Co., Ltd. (hereinafter "the Company") recognizes the importance of personal information protection and handles it appropriately in accordance with the following privacy policy.

1. Collection of Personal Information

In our recruitment activities, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Name, address, phone number, email address
  • Educational background, work history, qualifications and other career information
  • Skills and experience related to the position you are applying for

2. Purpose of Use of Personal Information

We use collected personal information only for the following purposes:

  • Implementation of recruitment selection
  • Communication and notification regarding recruitment
  • Human resource management after hiring

3. Use of Google Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics for the purpose of service improvement. Google Analytics analyzes user behavior using cookies, but does not include personally identifiable information.

If you do not wish to have information collected by Google Analytics, you can disable cookies in your browser settings or use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

We will not provide personal information to third parties without your consent, except as required by law.

5. Management of Personal Information

We maintain the accuracy of personal information and take appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, leakage, loss, damage, etc.

6. Contact Information

For inquiries regarding the handling of personal information, please contact:

NanoFrontier Co., Ltd.
Email: info@nanofrontier.jp

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may change this privacy policy in response to changes in laws and regulations. The revised privacy policy shall take effect when posted on this site.
