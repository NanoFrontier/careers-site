## 1. About Us

NanoFrontier is a research-driven startup that provides end-to-end solutions from design to mass production and quality assurance for materials in energy, environment, and pharmaceutical fields, centered around nanoparticle generation technology through reprecipitation method developed over many years at Tohoku University. We also provide nanoparticle manufacturing services and technology licensing, rapidly advancing joint research and PoC with manufacturers and academic institutions in Sendai (main base) and Tokyo (collaboration base).

---

## 2. Role & Responsibilities

Create a friction-free operation for month-end close, payments, evidence, contracts and approvals. Design workflows, ledgers and guidelines so that anyone can run them with the same quality. Operate tools (cloud accounting/HR/AI) with accuracy.

### What You'll Gain

- Build 0→1 back-office with clear rules, forms and tools
- Improve "how work gets done" across R&D/manufacturing/sales
- Embed AI/automation (template generation, input assist, reconciliation)
- Be the hub for grants/audit/tax and help build the company's foundation

---

## 3. Key Responsibilities

- Month-end ops: journal entries, payments, cash-in/out, AR/AP, expenses, fixed asset ledger
- Standardize closing: schedule, task breakdown, close playbook, CoA guideline
- Evidence/contract/approval flows: quote-PO-GR-invoice-payment linkage, contract registry, authority design, e-signature ops
- Grants/subsidies admin: evidence collection, report prep, settlement/audit materials (with owners)
- Audit/tax liaison: coordinate documents and responses
- Documentation: checklists, templates, and knowledge in Notion

---

## 4. Requirements, Skills & Experience

### Required

- Alignment with NanoFrontier's business and mission
- Experience in corporate accounting or accounting firms
- Experience in month-end closing, journal entries, and financial statement preparation
- Knowledge of accounting principles and tax regulations
- Japanese documentation, English reading comprehension

### Preferred

- Experience in startup or small company accounting
- Knowledge of cloud accounting systems and automation tools
- Experience in grant/subsidy administration and audit support
- Experience in process design and workflow optimization

### What We're Looking For

- Someone who can design clear processes and guidelines
- Someone who can implement automation and efficiency improvements
- Someone who can maintain accuracy while improving speed
