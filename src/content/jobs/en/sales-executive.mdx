## 1. About Us

NanoFrontier is a research-driven startup that provides end-to-end solutions from design to mass production and quality assurance for materials in energy, environment, and pharmaceutical fields, centered around nanoparticle generation technology through reprecipitation method developed over many years at Tohoku University. We also provide nanoparticle manufacturing services and technology licensing, rapidly advancing joint research and PoC with manufacturers and academic institutions in Sendai (main base) and Tokyo (collaboration base).

---

## 2. Role & Responsibilities

We need strong execution to convert early prospects into signed deals. You will run the full cycle from target prospecting to proposal, demo/sample coordination, PoC scoping, and closing. You will also help standardize quotes/pricing and SLAs, maintain CRM hygiene and forecast.

### What You'll Gain

- Build 0→1 sales motion directly (playbook/pricing)
- Work closely with technical teams on real use-cases
- Small team with fast decisions
- Possible adjunct position at Tohoku University via our Co-Creation Lab

---

## 3. Key Responsibilities

- Outbound/expo/referral prospecting and meeting creation
- Use-case based proposals/quotes, demo/sample coordination
- PoC proposal support (goals/KPIs/org/schedule)
- Keep CRM updated, next actions, and forecast
- Hit quarterly goals (new/existing, count and revenue)
- Gather VOC/competitor/pricing intel and feed back

---

## 4. Requirements, Skills & Experience

### Required

- Alignment with NanoFrontier's business and mission
- **3+ years B2B sales in chemicals/materials/equipment/semiconductors**
- Practical knowledge of requirements → quote → basic/individual contracts
- CRM pipeline operation and reporting
- Japanese (negotiation) and English (reading/writing)

### Preferred

- Joint research/grants (NEDO/JST) collaboration
- Basic knowledge of chemical regulations/SDS/export control
- Tradeshow ops, collaboration with inside sales/marketing
- Overseas customer/partner handling

### What We're Looking For

- Someone who can run short hypothesis → proposal → consensus cycles
- Someone who can record and share changes to avoid misses
- Someone who can manage themselves by numbers (margin/conversion/cycle time)
